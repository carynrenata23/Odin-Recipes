Watermelon Lemonade

Description

This watermelon lemonade is a light and refreshing summer drink that's easy to make with pureed watermelon and homemade lemonade. Garnish each glass with a slice of watermelon if you like.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 cups cold water
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups ice cubes

Direction

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all Ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Place watermelon into a blender; cover and puree until smooth. Strain through a fine mesh sieve.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bring sugar and 1/2 cup water to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in 3 cups of cold water and lemon juice.

  4. step 4

    5. Divide ice into 12 glasses; scoop 2 to 3 tablespoons of watermelon puree over ice, then top with lemonade.

  5. Step 5

    6. Gently stir before serving.
